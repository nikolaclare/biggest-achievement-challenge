Highlight Moments

Wildlife Encounters

Spotted kangaroos, was dived bombed by magpies (one particularly aggressive bird got me both ways and took a chunk out of my new helmet!) and was saved from riding over a large snake by an echidna wondering across the path.

Scenic Views

Both the scenery and artwork along the trail was stunning.

Unexpected Challenges

Encountered an unexpected detour due to a farmer blocking the trail to Alexandra. As dusk was approaching had to back track and head to Yea for the night which added kilometres to an already long ride.

This project is a responsive landing page built with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, created for the Biggest Achievement Challenge. The page highlights my personal achievement of getting back into cycling and completing the Great Victorian Rail Trail (GVRT) solo. The page showcases the multi-day cycling adventure, including key moments, fun facts, and a day-by-day breakdown of the journey.
